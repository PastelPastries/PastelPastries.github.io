About Us

Hi! We’re Sarah, Jayashree, and Sahasra — the trio behind Pastel Pastries, a small, sweet home bakery that specializes in cupcakes, cookies, brownies, and cake pops.

We started baking together just for fun, and it eventually turned into something we love. What began in our home kitchen has grown into a little business full of flavor and joy — where every batch is made with care.

But what makes Pastel Pastries extra special?

Most of the money we make goes to Ronald McDonald House Charities (RMHC), helping support families with sick children. Giving back is a big part of why we do what we do.

Everything we make is from scratch, in small batches, with love (and lots of sprinkles). Whether you’re ordering for a party, a gift, or just because, we’re so happy to bake for you!

Thanks for supporting our little bakery dream — and helping us support something even bigger.

With love,
The Pastel Pastries Team
