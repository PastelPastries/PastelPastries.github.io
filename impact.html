Baking with a Purpose

Where the Money Goes

Every dollar you spend on our sweets goes toward supporting Ronald McDonald House Charities (RMHC). We donate 100% of our proceeds to help families stay close to their hospitalized children.

Visit RMHC

Why RMHC?

RMHC provides housing, meals, and emotional support to families whose children are receiving treatment at hospitals — all at no cost. It's more than a place to stay; it’s a lifeline when people need it most.

Your Sweet Impact

With every cookie, brownie, or cupcake sold, you’re helping make life a little easier for families in crisis. Thanks to your support, we've already donated to bring smiles beyond our own kitchen!
